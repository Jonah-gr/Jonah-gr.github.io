## What it does

This ant food-search simulation provides an intuitive GUI to interact with the simulation environment in real time, letting you tweak parameters and observe the complex foraging behaviors that emerge from simple per-ant rules.

## How it works

Ants explore the environment, leave pheromone trails, and reinforce paths to food sources. Over time, the colony converges on efficient routes — a small but vivid demonstration of swarm intelligence.

## Why Kivy

Kivy gave us a cross-platform Python GUI with smooth real-time rendering, which made it easy to make the simulation interactive and visually engaging.
