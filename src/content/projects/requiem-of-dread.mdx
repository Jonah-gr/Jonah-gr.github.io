## The game

Embark on an adventure in the darkness and defeat enemies and bosses of increasing difficulty. Fight your way through three hand-crafted dungeon maps — from haunted graveyards to monster-filled forests to the empty tombs in the pyramids. Beware of what you'll find there.

The minimalist gameplay and retro pixel graphics make it easy to pick up, while the dark worlds reward exploration and persistence.

## Soundtrack

We composed a melodic horror soundtrack by hand to immerse the player in a world of death, destruction, and unease. The bosses do anything to keep you on your toes, so don't take too much time to enjoy their animations.

## Progression

The skill tree lets you spend collected coins on buffs. Open the skill tree, go on a shopping spree, and come back to finally kill the Grim Reaper.

## How it was built

Created in **Godot 4.3** by a group of four students. We had two months to learn and customize different techniques and ship this alpha version, including art, music, gameplay systems, and a Steam release.

## Stack

Godot 4.3, Python tooling, custom pixel art and original music.
